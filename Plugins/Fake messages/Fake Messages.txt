this plugin gives you the ability to create fake death/join/leave/sent message for or by a player!
with the colors and hoverable name and clickable /tell command! for example you can create these messages

Jeferson joined the game.
Jefferson left the game.
<Jeferson> Hi guys come to 1929 150 9192 I found a floating island
Jefferson was slain by Jeferson

stuff like that
